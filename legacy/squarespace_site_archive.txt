# How We Operate

Not a spa.
Not a salon.
A structured mobile dog grooming system built for calm dogs and consistent results.

**The dogs came first. The system came second.**

Every decision exists to produce clean dogs and predictable results.

## Built for Function

Most mobile grooming units rely on RV-style water pumps. They trickle.

We don't.

Our bathing system delivers water flow similar to what you expect from city water. That flow allows shampoo and rinse water to move fully through the coat instead of sitting on the surface.

Inside the trailer, the environment is controlled.

Oversized generators power true climate control. Air conditioning and heat are supported by a dedicated dehumidifier that maintains humidity around 30 percent regardless of what Florida is doing outside.

Hot and humid. Cold and rainy. Doesn't matter.

The dog stands in a stable, controlled environment from start to finish.

Powerful vacuums contain loose coat. High-output dryers move moisture efficiently so dogs can get back to their day.

Built for performance. Designed for dogs.

## The Hurricane Bath

We use a recirculating bathing system we call the Hurricane Bath.

Instead of applying shampoo by hand and hoping it works through the coat, the Hurricane Bath continuously circulates a shampoo and water mixture through the coat at strong, steady flow.

The movement lifts dirt, debris, and loose undercoat from deep within the coat while using less total water than traditional hand washing.

It is controlled. It is efficient. It is thorough.

The result is a cleaner coat and a better rinse without unnecessary product buildup.

A controlled storm that leaves the coat refreshed and balanced.

## Dogs We Specialize In

**We specialize in dogs that do not require haircuts.**

Short coats. Double coats. Heavy shedders. Working breeds.

We groom haircut breeds as well, but our core focus is efficient, low-stress grooming for natural coats.

When multiple dogs live together, we usually groom them together. Dogs live as a pack. Grooming is not a reason to separate them unless there is a practical reason to do so.

If they are calm together, they stay together. If one needs space to relax while the other is being worked on, we adjust.

The goal is always the same: reduce stress and keep the experience stable.

## Safety & Temperament

We groom friendly dogs.

For safety reasons, dogs must be able to be handled without aggression.

Snapping, attempting to bite, or aggressive behavior of any kind results in the appointment ending immediately.

Excessive resistance that prevents safe, efficient grooming may also result in service being declined.

This is not personal. It is a safety boundary. We are not willing to risk a dog bite injury that puts a dog groomer out of work.

If you have concerns about your dog's behavior, disclose them before booking so we can determine whether we are a good fit.

## Online Communication Only. No Phone Calls.

We do not take or return phone calls for any reason. We are working hands on with dogs throughout the day and cannot step away to answer or return calls.

All scheduling, updates, and questions are handled through our online system so everything stays documented and clear.

## Arrival Windows & Scheduling Commitment

Appointments are scheduled in arrival windows, not exact timestamps.

Grooming time varies based on coat condition, behavior, and real-world variables. Exact minute-by-minute predictions are not realistic in a mobile operation.

Before arrival, you will receive a heads-up text. Most of the time, that message includes a live Google Maps link so you can follow our progress toward your home.

You will know we are coming.

We operate by route and service area. Once the grace window passes, that time is reserved.

Your appointment time is reserved exclusively for you. Same-day cancellations are billed 100%.

### Clear pricing for dogs we're great at grooming.

Find your dog's category, confirm the price, and schedule when you're ready.

### Precision Nail Care starts at $30

We use a rotary tool to sculpt each nail to its ideal length and shape. This method allows the nail to be shaped shorter and finished smooth, leaving a rounded edge that reduces scratching and improves comfort.

If a dog is scheduled for full grooming, nail care for that dog is already included. Any additional dogs in your household that only need nail care are $15 each. No separate appointment needed. Just let us know when we arrive that you have additional dogs that only need nail care.

# Safety & Temperament

**We groom only friendly, non-aggressive dogs.**

If your dog is clearly friendly toward people and reasonably cooperative you can skip right to the button at the bottom of the page. Not sure? Keep reading.

Dogs that wiggle a little are all in a days work, but a dog that is excessively uncooperative to the point it can't be groomed safely needs some training before it will be eligible to schedule with us.

We have zero tolerance for dogs displaying aggressive behavior toward the groomer.

Snapping, biting, or attempting to bite will result in the appointment ending immediately.

Excessive resistance that prevents safe, efficient grooming may also result in service being declined.

We do not use muzzles. If a dog requires a muzzle for grooming, we are not the right fit.

We are not willing to risk a dog bite injury that puts a dog groomer out of work.

If you have concerns about your dog's behavior, disclose them before booking so we can determine whether we are a good fit.

Florida law holds dog owners responsible for bites, so we require dogs to be safe and non-aggressive.

# Service Area & Property Access

Our service is not available at **apartments**, **nursing homes**, **assisted living facilities**, or homes located on **unpaved roads**.

## Ocala Service Area Boundaries

- **North:** We do not travel north of Highway 326.
- **South:** We do not travel south of the Florida Greenway.
- **East:** We do not travel east of Baseline Road.
- **West:** We do not travel west of SW 110th Avenue.

If your address falls outside these boundaries, we are not able to schedule service.

## Property & Access Requirements

- My location is inside the service area on the map and description above.
- My home is on a paved road. (Unpaved driveways are fine.)
- I have space for a truck and trailer approximately two standard parking spaces long, or five standard parking spaces side by side if parking across spaces.
- If I live in a gated community, I am aware of the hours service providers are allowed to enter and will schedule within those hours.
- I understand apartments, nursing homes, and assisted living facilities are not eligible for service.
