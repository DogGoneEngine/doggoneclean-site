## Public showcase (the drop-in content)

### Power to spare
Dog Gone Clean runs on two heavy-duty generators, together enough to power a small house. Most of
that goes straight into what your dog actually feels: strong air conditioning, a dehumidifier that
keeps the trailer cool and dry, and high-velocity dryers.

### Cool and dry, whatever Florida is doing
Inside the trailer it stays cool, dry, and comfortable no matter the weather outside. (Pairs with
the language bank: the calm, familiar trailer, where thunder fades into the background.)

### Dried fast, the easy way on a dog
Drying is a one-two punch. The dehumidifier pulls moisture out of the air so it lifts off the
coat, and the high-velocity dryer pushes the rest off. Your dog is dry quickly and back to doing
what it does best.

### Why it matters
Plenty of power means nothing gets skimped: full climate control and full drying force, every
time.
